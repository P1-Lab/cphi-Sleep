  <p><strong>cphi-Sleep: Sovereign Natural Hybrid Sleep System</strong></p>

  <p>A clinical rejection of the "Foam Tax," fiberglass fire barriers, and petroleum-based bed-in-a-box mattresses.</p>

  <p>The cphi-Sleep system is a complete DIY blueprint for building a durable, non-toxic natural latex hybrid mattress using 100% natural materials: Dunlop & Talalay latex, individually pocketed coils, and organic wool.</p>

  <p><strong>Why This Matters</strong><br>
  Retail hybrids (Tempur-Pedic, Casper, Purple, etc.) routinely sell for $2,500–$4,500. Most are made of polyurethane foam that off-gasses VOCs, traps heat, and sags within 5–8 years, often with hidden fiberglass fire barriers.</p>

  <p>cphi-Sleep replaces marketing abstraction with deterministic engineering: renewable natural latex, breathable open-cell structure, zero motion transfer coils, and a safe natural wool fire barrier.</p>

  <p><strong>Key Advantages</strong></p>
  <p>- Lifespan: 15–20+ years with modular, replaceable layers.<br>
     - Comfort: Naturally cooling, responsive support with no excessive sink.<br>
     - Health: No petroleum foams, no fiberglass, no chemical flame retardants.<br>
     - Cost: Queen build ≈ $520–$720 depending on sourcing (see BOM.md).</p>

  <p><strong>Engineering Standards</strong></p>
  <p>- 100% natural GOLS-certified latex (Dunlop base + Talalay comfort layer)<br>

     - Organic wool batting as natural fire barrier<br>
     - Modular architecture for adjustable firmness and serviceability<br>
     - GOTS-certified organic cotton cover</p>
