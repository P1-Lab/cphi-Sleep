<div>
  <p><strong>Sourcing Guide</strong></p>

  <p><strong>Latex</strong></p>
  <p>
    Search: “100% natural Dunlop latex sheet” and “100% natural Talalay latex sheet”.<br>
    Recommended suppliers include Latex for Less, Sleep On Latex, or verified GOLS-certified manufacturers via reputable wholesale platforms. Prefer purchasing full slabs and cutting to size.<br>
    Avoid blended or ambiguously labeled “natural latex” products.
  </p>

  <p><strong>Pocketed Coils</strong></p>
  <p>
    Search: “Queen pocketed coil unit” or “Marshall coil core”.<br>
    Source from mattress component wholesalers, upholstery suppliers, or dedicated bedding manufacturers offering raw coil units.
  </p>

  <p><strong>Wool</strong></p>
  <p>
    Search: “organic wool batting mattress”.<br>
    Select dense, untreated batting from verified organic or ethically certified textile suppliers.
  </p>

  <p><strong>Cover</strong></p>
  <p>
    Search: “GOTS organic cotton zippered mattress cover”.<br>
    Naturepedic-style or equivalent certified organic encasements are appropriate reference standards.
  </p>

  <p><strong>Verification Standards</strong></p>
  <p>
    Prioritize GOLS certification for latex and GOTS certification for cotton and wool materials where available. Verify chain-of-custody and avoid ambiguous “natural” marketing claims without certification.
  </p>
</div>
