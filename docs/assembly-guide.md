  <p><strong>Assembly Guide: cphi-Sleep</strong></p>

  <p><strong>1. Preparation</strong></p>
  <p>
    - Verify all components match Queen size (60" x 80")<br>
    - Cut latex sheets to exact dimensions using a hot wire cutter. Cut slowly for clean edges and maintain adequate ventilation throughout the process
  </p>

  <p><strong>2. Layering (Bottom to Top)</strong></p>
  <p>
    1. Place pocketed coil core on a solid, evenly spaced slatted foundation<br>
    2. Install 3" Firm Dunlop latex layer directly over coil unit<br>
    3. Add 2" Talalay latex comfort layer on top<br>
    4. Fully wrap entire stack (coils + latex layers) in 1–1.5" organic wool batting on all sides and top surface<br>
    5. Insert completed assembly into GOTS-certified organic cotton zippered cover
  </p>

  <p><strong>Fitment Tip</strong><br>
  Partially zip cover first, then progressively work material into corners before completing closure.</p>

  <p><strong>3. Final Tuning & Maintenance</strong></p>
  <p>
    - Test firmness profile after initial assembly; latex layers may be flipped to adjust feel<br>
    - Rotate mattress 180° every 6 months to maintain even wear distribution<br>
    - Vacuum exterior cover monthly; spot clean with mild soap as needed<br>
    - Do not machine wash latex or wool components under any circumstances
  </p>
